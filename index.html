How to explore

  • Scroll / pinch — zoom in and out. Zoom past an edge to jump to the next cosmic scale.
  • Drag — orbit the view. Right-drag — pan.
  • Click any star, planet or galaxy to fly to it and read its NASA facts.
  • “Land on surface” in the info panel drops you onto a planet (where available).
  • Scale ladder (left) jumps between Earth → Solar System → Interstellar → Milky Way → Local Group → Observable Universe.
  • Search (top right) flies you straight to any named object.

Distances, sizes and facts are drawn from NASA / JPL public data. Scales are compressed for visibility — a true-to-scale universe would be mostly empty space.
